Culture Stock

Culture is the unspoken energy understood and spoken through out the room. The feng shui through a company or group of people.
For IT Citrine it’s a mixed bag of people. There are varying skill sets, skill levels, and motivations. The Physical setting is always in the EIT building although the location within the building varies. The ritual is the move from the main lecture room into another small class room for n intimate setting for hands on social and team development work. the space though expands into the many corners of Arkansas in that as a web-based class it touches wherever the student is willing to go. The stories are the laughs and the expansion of minds within the walls. The banter between the students in the lower left side of the building and that one guy in the very back and the instructor is lighthearted but rooted deep in a thirst for knowledge and a passion for providing. The chatter of excitement of things unfounded and discovered. The humor and play lend itself to this aspect as well. As learning while having fun makes the learning not feel so long. The language is the development and the understanding of the course at hand. The technical know and jargon is used interchangeably with common terms to be able to speak to a professional and explain it in simplistic terms for a client. All the Html in the world can’t help if you can’t communicate. This process is the development of tech speak, and industry know development.
To be honest culture is developed and evolves over time. The time in class develop some cultural traits but has not been around long enough to get a true sense of the concept of culture.
